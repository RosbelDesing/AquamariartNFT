About me

I consider that I do not have a personal style as an artist, I am constantly changing as a result of my learning process. I've been through several stages where my style varied from manga to portraits, but the central axis of my illustrations has always been women. The biggest change in my style happened along with my entry into the world of NFTs just 3 months ago, where I have since decided to dedicate myself more seriously to illustration. Possessed Witch

They say that deep in the woods lives a beautiful woman, but those who have seen her up close never returned home. Zyna was a young woman with a passion for sorcery, who lived in a cabin in the woods in the company of creatures she had summoned. At one point in her life, she decides to leave that forest, venture to start a new life and find her first love. She tried to get out, but the creatures had caged her. These beings that actually turned out to be evil were consuming his life energy.Zyna fell into an extreme madness, and the only solution she found was to try to bring that happiness she was looking for to her cabin

He began to walk through the woods looking for some other human. With her beautiful appearance she will attract several men to her cabin, but none of them will get out of there alive. Zyna ended up becoming a cursed being that fed on the life energy of humans. To this day, Zyna is still wandering the woods in search of that eternal love that she will never find.
